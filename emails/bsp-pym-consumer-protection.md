## Maging Smart Financial Consumer

HitPay is proud to support the Bangko Sentral ng Pilipinas (BSP) "Protect Your Money" (PYM) campaign, a nationwide initiative to help every Filipino make informed, confident financial decisions.

This campaign is a joint effort by the BSP, Insurance Commission, Philippine Deposit Insurance Corporation, and the Securities and Exchange Commission to protect consumers like you from scams, fraud, and unfair financial practices.

Here is what being a smart financial consumer means.

## #IKnowMyRights

As a financial consumer, you are entitled to fair and honest treatment from every institution you deal with.

::: bullets
**Right to Accurate Information**
All fees, rates, and terms must be disclosed before you sign or transact.
**Right to Fair Treatment**
Deceptive or abusive practices are prohibited.
**Right to Redress**
You have the right to file a complaint and receive a fair resolution.
**Protection from Harassment**
Threats and harassment in debt collection are illegal.
:::

## #IKnowMyOptions

There are more ways than ever to save, invest, and transact securely in the Philippines.

::: bullets
**Savings Accounts**
Basic Deposit Accounts at regulated banks — no maintaining balance required.
**Digital Transactions**
Pay and send money securely via BSP-regulated channels like InstaPay and PESONet.
**Investments**
Explore PERA accounts, government securities, and regulated investment products.
**Loans and Credit**
Compare rates and terms before borrowing. No surprises.
:::

---

## #IKnowTheRisks

Fraud and scams are getting more sophisticated. Stay one step ahead.

::: bullets
**Never Share Your OTP**
No legitimate bank or agency will ever ask for your One-Time Password.
**Protect Your Card Details**
Keep your card number, CVV, and PIN private at all times.
**Verify Before You Trust**
Check sender addresses carefully. When in doubt, go directly to the official website.
**Spot Investment Scams**
Unusually high returns are a red flag. Verify all investments through the SEC first.
:::
