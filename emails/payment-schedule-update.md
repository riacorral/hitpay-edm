Hi there,

We're writing to inform you of an important update to how we handle settlements for your ecommerce card transactions.

**Starting today, online card payments will follow a T+7 settlement schedule. Funds will be available 7 business days after the transaction date.**

For clarity, non-card transactions will remain on a T+1 settlement timeline.

---

## Why We're Making This Change

Online card transactions carry higher fraud risk than in-person payments. Unlike card-present sales where partners are physically at your store, ecommerce payments are more vulnerable to fraud, stolen cards, and chargebacks.

**Here's what we do to protect you:** We absorb the costs of fraud monitoring, chargeback management, and dispute resolution. When a fraudulent transaction occurs, we handle it. You don't get charged back or lose money you've already received. This protection isn't free, and the T+7 window gives us the necessary time to identify and block suspicious transactions before funds reach your account.

---

## What This Means for You

- You remain protected from chargeback losses on ecommerce sales
- We continue managing fraud and disputes at no additional cost to you
- Your in-store (card-present) transactions are unaffected and maintain their current schedule

---

We know cash flow matters to your business. This change helps us maintain the fraud protection you rely on, without passing those costs to you through higher fees or chargeback penalties.
